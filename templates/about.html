About Ketogenic Diet


A keto or ketogenic diet is a low-carb, moderate protein, higher-fat diet that can help you burn fat more effectively. It has many benefits for weight loss, health, and performance, as shown in over 50 studies. That’s why it’s recommended by so many doctors. A keto diet can be especially useful for losing excess body fat without hunger and for improving type 2 diabetes.

How to go about it?


What’s the most important thing to do to reach ketosis? Avoid eating too many carbs. You’ll likely need to keep carb intake under 50 grams of net carbs per day, ideally below 20 grams. The fewer the carbs, the more effective the diet appears to be for reaching ketosis, losing weight or improving type 2 diabetes. Counting carbs can be helpful at first. But if you stick to our recommended foods and recipes you can stay keto even without counting.
